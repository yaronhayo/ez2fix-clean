The First Opener Installation in Union City

The Problem:

Lisa's Union City home had a manual garage door that required getting out of the car in all weather. As a working mother with small children, she needed the convenience and security of an automatic opener.

Our Diagnosis:

We evaluated her 8-foot-high garage door and determined it needed a 1/2 HP belt-drive opener for smooth, quiet operation. We also recommended adding safety features including photo-eye sensors and rolling code remotes.

We installed a Chamberlain B970 belt-drive opener with battery backup, programmed multiple remotes and keypad, and installed safety sensors. Lisa now has reliable, quiet operation with security lighting and backup power during outages.

Frequently Asked Questions About Opener Installation

Get answers to common questions about garage door opener installation and smart technology.

What type of garage door opener should I choose?

The best opener depends on your garage size, door weight, and desired features. Belt drives are quietest, chain drives are most affordable, and smart openers offer remote control and integration with home automation systems. We'll recommend the best option during our consultation.

How long does opener installation take?

Most opener installations take 2-4 hours depending on the complexity. This includes mounting the opener, installing safety features, programming remotes, and testing all functions. Smart opener setup with WiFi may require additional time.

Can I install an opener on my existing garage door?

Most garage doors can be fitted with an opener, but we need to assess the door's condition, balance, and hardware. Some older doors may need spring adjustments or hardware upgrades for safe automatic operation.

What smart features are available with modern openers?

Modern smart openers offer WiFi connectivity, smartphone apps, voice control (Alexa/Google), battery backup, LED lighting, and integration with security systems. You can monitor and control your garage door from anywhere in the world.

What warranty comes with opener installation?

We provide a comprehensive 15-year warranty covering the opener motor and our installation work. This includes parts replacement and labor for any installation-related issues. Manufacturer warranties may provide additional coverage.
